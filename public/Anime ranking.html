My top 3 anime of all time

Demon Slayer

It boasts stunning animation, a compelling story with strong emotional depth, well-developed characters, exciting fight sequences, and a unique blend of action and poignant themes

Attack on titan

It offers a captivating story with complex characters, a unique world-building concept where humans live within walls defending against giant, man-eating Titans, thrilling action sequences with stunning animation, deep thematic exploration of war and morality, and a plot full of twists and turns that keep you hooked until the very end.

Naruto shippuden

It's a classic anime with a compelling story about overcoming adversity, featuring well-developed characters, exciting ninja battles, and deep themes of friendship, family, and redemption, all wrapped up in a vibrant world of ninjas and unique abilities
